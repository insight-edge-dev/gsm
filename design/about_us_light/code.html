Our Legacy in Coal

GSM Group was born from the rugged terrains of the energy sector. Starting as a specialized coal trading enterprise, we mastered the complexities of global supply chains and bulk commodity logistics.

This foundational experience taught us the value of reliability, precise timing, and international market dynamics—principles that continue to guide us as we venture into AI-driven logistics and renewable energy infrastructures.
